Overview

The Home Elite 3G Signal Booster is an excellent choice to cover areas of up to 300 square metres. This is a perfect signal booster for enhancing 3G signals in small homes, small offices, or any other smaller area of 300 square metres. It supports 3G signal enhancement for all major UK networks like Vodafone, EE, iD, O2, Three, Virgin, Tesco, GiffGaff, BT, etc. This booster can serve 6 users simultaneously. .
