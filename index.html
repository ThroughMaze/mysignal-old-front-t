How it works

Step 1: Receive

The external antenna captures weak cell signals from nearby towers, gathering all available network connections in your area.

Step 2: Amplify

Our powerful signal amplifier boosts the captured signal up to 32x, significantly enhancing its strength and quality.

Step 3: Broadcast

The inside antenna distributes the amplified signal throughout space, providing strong, reliable coverage to all your mobile devices.
