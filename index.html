How it works

1. Install External Antenna

Mount the external antenna in an area with the strongest signal, typically on your roof or outside wall.

2. Connect the Booster

Place the signal booster unit inside your building and connect it to the external antenna using the provided cable.

3. Position Internal Antenna

Install the internal antenna in the area where you need improved signal coverage and enjoy enhanced connectivity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Signal boosters work by capturing weak cellular signals from outside, amplifying them, and then rebroadcasting the stronger signal inside your building. This process involves three main components: an external antenna, an amplifier unit, and an internal antenna.
Yes, our signal boosters are designed to work with all major UK carriers including EE, Vodafone, O2, Three, and many others. They boost all cellular frequencies used by these networks.
Coverage area depends on the specific model and the strength of the outside signal. Our boosters range from 300 SQM for residential use up to 5000 SQM for large commercial buildings. The stronger the outside signal, the larger the coverage area.
