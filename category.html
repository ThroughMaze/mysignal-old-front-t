What are Home Signal Boosters?

Home signal boosters are devices designed to amplify weak cellular signals within residential spaces. They work by capturing the existing outdoor signal, amplifying it, and then redistributing the enhanced signal throughout your home.

Our range of home signal boosters are perfect for:

  • Small to medium-sized homes (up to 500 SQM)
  • Improving call quality and preventing dropped calls
  • Enhancing mobile data speeds for faster browsing
  • Supporting multiple devices and users simultaneously

Frequently Asked Questions

Home signal boosters work by using three main components: an external antenna, an amplifier, and an internal antenna. The external antenna captures the existing cellular signal from outside your home, the amplifier boosts this signal, and the internal antenna redistributes the enhanced signal throughout your home. This creates a stronger, more reliable connection for all your mobile devices.

Most of our home signal boosters are designed to work with all major UK carriers, including Vodafone, O2, EE, and Three. However, some models are optimized for specific carriers or frequencies. We recommend checking the product specifications or using our "Find the Right Booster" tool to ensure you select a booster that's compatible with your carrier.

The improvement you'll experience depends on several factors, including the strength of the existing signal outside your home, the construction materials of your building, and the specific booster model. Typically, our customers report a 2-5 bar increase in signal strength, which translates to significantly clearer calls, fewer dropped calls, and faster data speeds. In optimal conditions, our boosters can amplify signals by up to 32 times their original strength.

Most of our home signal boosters are designed for easy DIY installation, with comprehensive instructions included. The typical installation takes about 30-60 minutes and requires basic tools. However, for optimal performance, especially in larger homes or complex installations, we do offer professional installation services. This service includes signal testing, optimal placement of components, and ensuring maximum coverage throughout your home.
